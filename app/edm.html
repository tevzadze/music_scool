Course plan

1. Musical theory

In this part of the course you will learn the fundamental aspects of music theory. We will start by building different chords, so you’ll understand what root-note is, how to pick it up and build your chords. You will get the knowledge of melody structures and find out how different hit tunes are made in different genres. You will also learn how to get a right emotion, character, and color to your melodies and harmonies. You will learn what scales and frets are and how they can be used in your production.

2. Arrangement

These lessons will help you to better understand how electronic music is made in general. We will analyze a variety of EDM tracks and figure out the arrangements from the inside out. You are going to learn how to choose the right sounds, how to make your arrangements more dynamic and interesting with the help of various effects.

3. Mixing

This part of the course was designed to learn the mixing techniques. You will learn different types of EQ and how to use them. You will also learn more about the side-chain compression technique. The lesson about panning and audio-space will help you understand the balance in your mix correctly and how Haas effect can help you make your instruments sound «wider». Alex will also teach you Compression and everything about it, including methods and techniques.

4. Mastering

Here you will learn the basics of mastering and how to prepare your mix to go out in the world. In this part, we will analyze various plugins on the master channel and figure out what is better for your mix and how to export your tracks properly.
